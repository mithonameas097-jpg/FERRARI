Daytona SP3

The Ferrari Daytona SP3 is an Icona Series masterpiece inspired by Ferrari's legendary 1960s endurance racers. Powered by a naturally aspirated 6.5-litre V12 producing 840 cv, it blends timeless styling with modern engineering to deliver an unforgettable driving experience.

Icona Design

The Daytona SP3 features a lightweight carbon-fibre chassis and body inspired by Ferrari's sports prototypes. Its flowing surfaces, dramatic proportions and aerodynamic efficiency create a design that is both timeless and unmistakably Ferrari.

Naturally Aspirated V12

The Daytona SP3 is powered by Ferrari's 6.5-litre naturally aspirated V12, delivering 840 cv to the rear wheels through a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission. The result is an emotional soundtrack and thrilling performance without hybrid assistance.
