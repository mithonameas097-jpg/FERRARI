12 Cilindri Spider

The Ferrari 12Cilindri Spider is a luxurious front-engine convertible grand tourer that celebrates Ferrari's legendary V12 heritage. Unveiled in 2024, it is powered by a naturally aspirated 6.5-liter V12 engine producing 830 horsepower, delivering thrilling performance and a distinctive engine sound. Its retractable hardtop opens in just 14 seconds, allowing drivers to enjoy the excitement of open-air driving without sacrificing comfort or style. Featuring elegant design, advanced technology, and exceptional handling, the 12Cilindri Spider offers the perfect combination of luxury, performance, and everyday usability.

Monofuselage

Revuelto introduces a revolutionary carbon-fiber monofuselage inspired by aeronautics, extending the monocoque to the front end.
Made with Forged Composites, it improves sustainability while reducing weight, increasing stiffness beyond 40,000 Nm/° and enhancing occupant protection.

Powertrain

Revuelto redefines Lamborghini engineering with an all-new hybrid V12. Rotated 180° and redesigned for electrification, it features advanced
combustion, intake and exhaust systems, reduced weight and higher efficiency. Combined with three e-motors, it delivers 1015 PS and exceeds 350 km/h.
